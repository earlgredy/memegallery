Welcome to Meme Gallery

Meme is a term introduced in British biologist Dawkins' book Selfish Gene as a unit of transmission of non-genetic cultural elements or cultures that can be stored in an individual's memory like a gene or replicated in another individual's memory. In the delivery of culture, intermediate intermediates that act as duplicates like genes are needed, and the units, forms, types, and elements of information that play this role are memes. Another example of a meme is that all cultural phenomena fall within the scope of the meme and one person's good deeds or evil deeds are transmitted to several people and influenced.
